This page will contain the definition and flag

Androgynesexual, androgynosexual or gynandrosexual describes a person who has sexual attraction exclusively towards androgynes and/or people of an androgynous gender- people who are simultaneously masculine and feminine or simultaneously men and women. It can also be described as someone who is attracted to both men and women, particularly those with an androgynous appearance. Under this definition it is a combination of androsexual and gynesexual, with elements of linsexual.
